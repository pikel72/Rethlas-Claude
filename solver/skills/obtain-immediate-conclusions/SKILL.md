# Obtain Immediate Conclusions

## When to use

When starting a new problem, branch, or subgoal — before `$direct-proving`.

## Output

Append to `immediate_conclusions`; `$record-keeping` covers field shapes.

## Steps

1. Normalize notation and restate equivalently.
2. List direct consequences from definitions.
3. Split necessary vs. sufficient conditions.
4. Flag fragile ones; suggest `$construct-counterexamples` next.
